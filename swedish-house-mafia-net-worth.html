What is Swedish House Mafia's Net Worth?

Swedish House Mafia is a Swedish electronic dance trio who a net worth of $100 million dollars. Originating in Stockholm, Sweden in 2005, the group consists of members Axwell, Steve Angello, and Sebastian Ingrosso. the music is comprised of an arrangement of sequencers, synthesizers, samplers, drum machines, computers, turntables, keyboards, and Logic Pro. They were affiliated with record labels such as UMG, Polydor, and Ministry of Sound Australia.

The group disbanded in 2013.
