What Was Brandon Lee's Net Worth at the Time of His Death?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 27, 2024

Celebrity

Alec Baldwin's prop gun incident on the set of 'Rust' is reminding everyone of the tragic death of Bruce Lee's son, Brandon Lee. Brandon Lee died while filming for 'The Crow.' Here's Brandon Lee's net worth at the time of his death.

Published on October 22, 2021

2 min read

Article Highlights:

  • Bruce Lee’s son, Brandon Lee, died when a “lethal obstruction” was accidentally shot into his abdomen while filming on the set of The Crow
  • Brandon Lee’s net worth stood around $5 million at the time of his death
  • Brandon Lee’s family released a Twitter statement about Alec Baldwin’s prop gun incident

Alec Baldwin shot a prop gun on the set of his new film, Rust, that accidentally killed a person on the set. Sadly, this incident reminds moviegoers of what happened to Bruce Lee’s son, Brandon Lee. Brandon Lee’s death shocked Hollywood, as it happened accidentally while filming on the set of The Crow in 1993. But prior to the accident, Lee had career fans won’t forget. Here is Brandon Lee’s net worth at the time of his death.

Brandon Lee’s death happened on the set of ‘The Crow’

Bruce Lee’s son, Brandon Lee, took after his famous father. He was also a martial artist and fight choreographer, but sadly, he died at just 28 years old due to an accident on the set of The Crow.

According to the Los Angeles Times, the first article about Lee’s death was released on April 1, 1993. The article explained “a small explosive charge used to simulate gunfire went off inside a grocery bag during filming” of the movie. Later on, another report noted Lee died via “a .44-caliber bullet” that was lodged in his spine.

Ultimately, Lee’s mother, Linda Lee Cadwell, filed a lawsuit regarding the incident. The lawsuit detailed how Lee died when a co-star fired blank ammunition, which “propelled” a “lethal obstruction” into Lee’s abdomen. The obstruction was “lodged in the barrel of the gun during the filming of another scene several weeks earlier.” The case was ruled an accident.

What was Brandon Lee’s net worth when he died?

Bruce Lee’s son, Brandon Lee, had a bright future ahead of him in film. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Brandon Lee’s net worth was around $5 million when he died.

The publication notes Lee was born in 1965 and starred in movies like Crime Killer, Legacy of Rage, Laser Mission, Showdown in Little Tokyo, Rapid Fire, and, of course, The Crow. Additionally, he choreographed the fighting in a number of films, The Crow included. In 1995, he was nominated for an MTV Movie Award for Best Male Performance thanks to his work in The Crow. Additionally, in 1986 the star received a nomination for the Hong Kong Film Award for Best New Performer.

His family reacted to Alec Baldwin’s prop gun incident that killed 1 person

Bruce Lee’s son’s family reacted via Twitter after Alec Baldwin’s prop gun incident. Brandon Lee’s sister reportedly released a tweet from his official Twitter account.

“Our hearts go out to the family of Halyna Hutchins and to Joel Souza and all involved in the incident on Rust,” Fox News reports the tweet reads. “No one should ever be killed by a gun on a film set. Period.”

Brandon Lee’s Twitter account has nearly 20,000 followers in 2021. And many fans reacted to the tweet with additional condolences to all those affected by what occurred on the set of Rust.
