What is Jason Nevins' Net Worth?

Jason Nevins is an American songwriter, record producer, and remixer who has a net worth of $8 million. Jason Nevins was born in Los Angeles, California in December 1972. As an artist he released his debut studio album Red/Green CD in 1993. Nevins has also released the albums Uni-Vs-Al in 2000, Pushing It Hard in 2003, The Funk Rocker in 2005, and Pushing It Harder: The Lost Tapes in 2006. One of his best known songs was "Its Like That" by Run-DMC vs.

Jason Nevins which he produced and mixed. The song went multi-platinum and Nevins also had a lot of success with the song "Cruise" by Florida Georgia Line featuring Nelly which he produced. He has also produced, mixed, or written for other songs including "Popular Song" by Mika featuring Ariana Grande, "I'm in Heaven" by Jason Nevins featuring Holly James, and more. He has also worked on compilations including High School Musical 2: Non-Stop Dance Party and Jersey Shore Soundtrack.
