Alexus Oladi

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 22, 2024

Who's Alexus Oladi?

Alexus Oladi who was born on July 4, 2007 is an American professional dancer and reality star. She gained fame for appearing on the sixth and seventh seasons of the Lifetime "Dance Moms" reality competition. The dancer also had her own YouTube web show "Giggles Talk."

Who are Alexus Oladi's parents?

The star was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah, where she lives to this day. Her parents's names are Tiffanie and Justin. Tiffanie is passionate about dancing, too. She is available on Instagram under the handle dancemom_tiffanie. Alexus also has a twin brother named Alex. He was a frequent guest on "Giggles Talk". She is very close to all her family members and often uploads photos of them on her Instagram page.

Dancing career

Alexus started her career as a dancer when she was only two years old. She also was doing gymnastics, but stopped because she decided to focus solely on dancing. By the age of eight, Alexus had already become a national champion several times and won 'Mini Female Dancer' award. She trained in countless dance styles, including tap, modern, jazz, ballet and hip-hop. From 2012 to 2016, the dancer won more then ten awards and six scholarships. In January 2016, Alexus joined the new mini team in the sixth season of the "Dance Mom" reality show. She continued to be a part of the team until the second episode of the seventh season, when it was disbanded. Alexus continued to pursue a career as a dancer and is also considering becoming a dance teacher. She also modeled for Jo and Jax dancewear company.

Is Alexus Oladi in a relationship?

The "Dance Moms" star is currently single and focusing on her career.

How much is Alexus Oladi's net worth?

The dancer's net worth is estimated at around $0.3 - 1.5 million as of 2022.

Facts

  • She is friends with many of her "Dance Moms" co-stars, including Maddie Ziegler, Brady Farrar, Peyton Evans and Elliana Walmsley.
  • Alexus's nickname is Giggles.
  • Her favorite Disney movie is Mulan.
  • In 2017, Alexus appeared in a music video for aspiring singer Azalea Carey's song "Extra."
