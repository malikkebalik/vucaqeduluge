John Deere GX355 Specs, Price, Review, Attachments 2022

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

John Deere GX355 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers, Attachments, Features & Pictures

John Deere GX355 Garden Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 2V-78 diesel 2-cylinder liquid-cooled vertical-shaft V-Twin engine, which delivers 18 hp (13.4 kW) horsepower.

John Deere GX355 Garden Tractor featured an 18HP Yanmar 749cc 2-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, power steering, wet disc brakes, 742 lbs weight, infinite forward and reverse gears, and 5.0 Qts 4.7 L oil capacity.

John Deere GX355 Garden tractor attachments include a 48″ mid-mount mower deck, 54″ mid-mount mower deck, snowblower, and blade.
