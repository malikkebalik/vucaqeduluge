Is Oti Mabuse Pregnant Where Is She Today? Husband And Family Details

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

The winner of Strictly Come Dancing season 2019, Oti Mabuse is not pregnant. The dancer was rumored to be pregnant with her present partner Marius but all those rumors turn out to be false.

Oti has been happily married to her long-term partner Marius. The couple tied the knot on December 2014.

Despite them not having any kids yet, their rumors pop up over the internet from time to time.

The dancing duo is constantly seen together and runs a small dance studio together in the heart of the UK, London.

Is Oti Mabuse Pregnant?

Mabuse has not ever been pregnant in her life. However many questions regarding her pregnancy often arise among her fans.

One of the reasons that she had to face the such question is her husband’s suspicious post.

Her husband once posted a cute photo of themselves and captioned it, “Psst… can you keep a secret? Link in bio.” This quickly lets her fans think that she was pregnant. 

Although the dancer made it clear within the comments that it wasn’t the case but she couldn’t stop the storm.

Recently upon asking what her future family plan is, Otlile replied that she still has many things left to do.

Also recently, popular comedian Jimmy Carr jokingly said that Otlile Mabuse is pregnant with his baby after they danced together in his show.

This made Mabuse’s mother even believe the story after seeing it in some random new article. Later she explained that it was just sarcasm.

Her different wishes include hosting her own dance reality show and traveling around the world which she is currently doing. The couple is frequently seen traveling to different parts of the world together.

Where is she now?

Otlile Mabuse was born on the 8th of august 1990 and is currently 31 years of age. The South African currently lives in the United Kingdom.

She was born in Pretoria, South Africa.
