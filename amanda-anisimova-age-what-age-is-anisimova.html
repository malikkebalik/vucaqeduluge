Amanda Anisimova age: What age is Anisimova?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Russian-born Amanda Anisimova is a rising tennis star who was raised in America and now competes internationally.

She is the youngest athlete on the Women’s Tennis Association (WTA) top 100 list at the age of 17.

She recently experienced one of her life’s most significant moments when she won her maiden WTA championship at the Copa Colsanitas on April 29, 2019.

Amanda is frequently compared to tennis greats like Elise Mertens, Aryna Sabalenka, Kiki Bertens, Petra Martic, and Andrea Petkovic because of her professionalism.

In New Jersey, where she was born, Olga Anisimova and Konstantin Anisimov gave birth to Amanda Anisimova on August 31, 2001.

The couple had travelled from Russia to the United States before she was born to provide their children access to more opportunities.

Amanda’s older sister Maria, who went to college business school and played collegiate tennis for the University of Pennsylvania, is the only other kid in the family. Unexpectedly, their parents had never met at a sporting event and had careers in banking and finance.
