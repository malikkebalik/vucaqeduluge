All About Linus Tech Tips YouTuber Linus Sebastian

Linus Sebastian (age: 37, August 20, 1986) is a prominent Canadian YouTuber known for his YouTube channel, Linus Tech Tips. On this channel, he offers valuable tech tips and provides product reviews to his substantial audience of 15 million subscribers.

Prior to his YouTube career, Linus was actively involved in presenting technology-related videos for the now-defunct Canadian computer retailer NCIX from 2007 to 2013. Additionally, he is the founder and former Chief Executive Officer of Linus Media Group (LMG).

However, as of July 1, 2023, Linus Sebastian stepped down from his position as CEO of LMG and assumed the role of Chief Vision Officer. Linus’ success can be attributed to his distinctive approach to conveying tech knowledge.

Nevertheless, his recent endeavors have faced scrutiny, raising concerns about his content’s quality and expertise in the field.

This has led to a surge in controversy and criticism surrounding Linus Sebastian, prompting further investigation into the specifics of these issues.

Linus Tech Tips Attracted Controversy

On August 14, Linus Tech Tips was embroiled in controversy following a video posted by Gamers Nexus, another technology review channel boasting 2 million subscribers.

Gamers Nexus’ video has garnered 3.8 million views and raises concerns about inaccuracies in Linus Tech Tips’ reviews of graphics processing units (GPUs) and central processing units (CPUs).

Linus Tech Tips has acknowledged some of these inaccuracies. Additionally, Gamers Nexus presented employee testimonials alleging that Linus Tech Tips prioritizes quantity over quality in its content production, rushing to release videos.

Steve Burke, from Gamers Nexus, accused Linus Tech Tips of failing in its “responsibility to do due diligence on a product.”

Burke pointed to a water cooling block that Linus Tech Tips purportedly tested with an incompatible GPU and subsequently labeled as a “bad product.” Burke argued that this evaluation could potentially harm the manufacturer, Billet Labs, a small company.

In response to these allegations, Linus Sebastian, the former CEO of Linus Tech Tips, acknowledged the company’s experience of “growing pains” and pledged to focus on enhancing the quality of their work.

However, he defended his decision not to re-test the Billet Labs product with a compatible GPU, asserting that it wouldn’t have altered the conclusion that the product is an “egregious waste of money.”

Steve Burke criticized this response as “unhinged” and “unapologetic.” Furthermore, Madison Reeve, a former social media manager at Linus Tech Tips, revealed disturbing allegations in a Twitter thread.

She claimed to have been “inappropriately grabbed multiple times in the office,” subjected to sexually and demeaning comments from coworkers, and saw her mental health deteriorate to the point where she resorted to self-harm to avoid going to work.

Linus Tech Tips’ CEO, Terren Tong, expressed shock upon learning of Reeve’s allegations and pledged to conduct a thorough review through human resources and an external investigator.

Linus Sebastian’s Early Life and Education

On August 20, 1986, Linus Sebastian (age 37) was born in Ladner, British Columbia, Canada, although he grew up on a farm in Maple Ridge. Linus has four siblings, including two brothers named George and Asher, and two sisters, namely Emily and Isabelle.

Growing up in Maple Ridge, Sebastian attended Garibaldi Secondary School. According to one of his videos, he has had a special connection with computers and technology since the beginning but did not know that one day it would become his passion.

Linus Sebastian’s Career and Net Worth

Linus Sebastian’s career began at the Canadian online computer store NCIX, where he was initially asked to host the company’s technology channel to showcase various products.

During this time, he had the support of an anonymous cameraperson and editor and operated with limited resources, often using a camera borrowed from the son of the company’s president.

In the early stages of the technology channel, faced with high production costs and relatively low viewership, Sebastian was directed to create the Linus Tech Tips channel as a more cost-effective extension of the NCIX channel. This move allowed for lower production values without negatively impacting the NCIX brand.

Linus Tech Tips was established on November 24, 2008. Subsequently, due to a disagreement regarding company management, Linus Sebastian decided to depart from NCIX and founded Linus Media Group (LMG).

As part of this transition, he negotiated an agreement that allowed him to retain ownership of Linus Tech Tips under the umbrella of LMG while also agreeing to a non-compete clause.

In September 2021, Linus Sebastian revealed a personal investment of nearly $225,000 in Framework Computer, a manufacturer specializing in modular laptops.

Then, on May 18, 2023, he announced his decision to resign as Chief Executive Officer at Linus Media Group. Effective July 1, 2023, he would transition into a creative role as Chief Vision Officer. It’s worth noting that he had previously contemplated retiring from YouTube in January 2020.
