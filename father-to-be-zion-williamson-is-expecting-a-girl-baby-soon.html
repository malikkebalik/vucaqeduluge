Father-To-Be Zion Williamson Is Expecting A Girl Baby Soon!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 29, 2024

Zion Williamson will have a daughter. The star of the Pelicans is having a girl with his lover, whose name is Ahkeema. The happy couple had a party on June 3 to find out the gender of the baby. According to her Instagram and a video she shared Tuesday on YouTube, the party was held in a fancy house.

Below we have provided every social media post they did from the gender reveal ceremony with a caption along the posts. Williamson and Ahkeema can be seen cheering in the four-minute video as pink fireworks go off behind a sign that says

“Boy or Girl.” “June 3rd, I invited all my family and close friends to help us celebrate 🎉 our new addition to our family,” Ahkeema wrote on her Instagram page, which identifies her as a video creator named Rose.

Rose is also listed as her name on her YouTube page. The gender reveal party was in the garden of the house. There was a chef-prepared dinner, a bar, baby games, and many blue and pink treats. The couple’s guests ate under white tents while the couple sat at their table with a heart-shaped rose decoration and a sonogram picture. They also gave food bags to thank their guests for coming.

“You’re going to see this at some point,” Williamson said at the beginning of the video. “I don’t know what the future holds, but mommy and daddy love you. “If you don’t know nothing else in this world, know that mommy and daddy love you for life.”

Ahkeema looked at Williamson and said, “Aw, you’re so sweet.” The former first-round pick was also wearing a shirt with a funny saying on the back. , “I’m here because I don’t want to get yelled at.”

Ahkeema posted a collection of pictures from the gender reveal party, where she wore a beautiful white dress and then switched to a black one.

Williamson is pretty private about his personal life, so he didn’t post about his pregnancy or share his gender on any social media site. It’s unclear when or how the Jordan Brand player, who is 22, and Ahkeema got together. From what her Instagram says, she has her own real estate business.
