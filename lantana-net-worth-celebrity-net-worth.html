What is Lantana's Net Worth?

Lantana is an American rap artist who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Lantana, also known as Marcus Waldon, was born in Cincinnati, Ohio, and began releasing tracks and mixtapes under his own label, All Hustle No Luck. He was subsequently invited to perform as part of the 2012 Beats Summer Concert Series, an annual music series underwritten by Pepsi. He went on to catch the attention of James Rosemund, a hip-hop manager based on the East Coast, who grew up in the music industry as the son of famed music producer, Jimmy "Henchman" Rosemund. The younger Rosemund is known for fostering new talent. The release of Lantana's mixtape, "Lantana On the Way", which featured the hit single "All Hustle No Luck", proved to be a step up to the next level for the artist. In April 2013, he signed with Polo Grounds Music, a subsidiary of RCA Records.
