Blake Moynes Bio, Age, Height, Parents, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 20, 2024

Blake Moynes is a handsome Canadian television personality and a wildlife manager. He gained fame as a contestant in season 16 of The Bachelorette where he tried to impress contestants like Tayshia and Clare. Blake won The Bachelorette Season 16 of Katie Thurston but later parted their ways. Currently, Blake is finding his better half as he entered the Bachelor in Paradise Season 29. 

Early Life

Blake Moynes was born on 9th August 1990 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. He has been secretive about his family information. Blake has never revealed the name of his father nor any information related to their occupation but he once revealed that his mother’s name is Emily.

He is known to have a sister named Taylor Moynes and a brother named Cody Moines. As for his education, Blake attended Notre Dame Secondary School and completed his high school later. After his high school graduation, Blake enrolled at the Algonquin College of Applied Arts and Technology.

He received his degree and majored in Outdoor Adventure. After graduating in 2011, he attended Thompson Rivers University in 2012 from where he studied Adventure Tourism Management and Outdoor Education, and graduated from there in 2014.

Blake Moynes Career

Blake currently works in the wildlife field as a wildlife manager for an organization but he has not disclosed its name. He is a television star who appeared on the television reality series The Bachelorette to find his love.
