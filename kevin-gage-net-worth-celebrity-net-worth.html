What is Kevin Gage's Net Worth?

Kevin Gage is an American actor who has a net worth of $300 thousand. Kevin Gage was born in Wisconsin in 1959 and made his on-screen debut in 1984's "Billy Crystal: A Comic's Line". Gage appeared in a handful of shows and movies in the late '80s and early '90s before landing the part of Waingro in 1995's "Heat". He went on to appear in "Nash Bridges", "Con Air", "G.I. Jane", "Double Tap", "Point Blank", "Martial Law", "Going to California", "American Girl", "Smallville", "Lightning Bug", "CSI: Miami", "Love's Abiding Joy", "The Nine", "Cosmic Radio", "Chasing 3000", "Exact Bus Fare", "Born That Way", "Laid to Rest", "The Killing Jar", "Happiness Runs", "Call of Duty: Ghosts", and "Fear Clinic".

Gage has made headlines for his personal life. In 1985, he married American actress and ex-model Kelly Preston, though they divorced two years later in 1987. In the early 2000s, Gage was arrested for cultivating marijuana, and he received a 41-month prison sentence for the charges despite having a license for medical marijuana in California. Gage claimed he grew marijuana to help him deal with injuries stemming from a car accident nearly a decade earlier. He married his second wife, Shannon Perris-Knight, in 2006 after his prison time was behind him.
