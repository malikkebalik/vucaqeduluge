Joey King Wiki, Age, Net Worth, Boyfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Joey Lynn King, popularly known as Joey King, is an American who is a popular actress and a well-known model by profession. She is in the industry since 2006 and since then has had a lot of fans. She has been nominated for various prestigious awards like Golden Globe Awards, Primetime Emmy Award and also has won a lot.

She has appeared in some famous movies titled ‘The Lie’ from 2018, ‘Slender Man’ from 2018, ‘Wish Upon’ from 2017, ‘Independence Day: Resurgence’ from 2017, ‘White House down’ from 2013, ‘The Conjuring’ from 2013, ‘Oz the Great and Powerful’ from 2013, and many more.
Joey King Wiki/Biography

Born on 30 July 1999, Joey King’s age is 22 years as of May 2021. She was born and raised in a well-settled family from Los Angeles, California, the United States. She is known to hold an American nationality and believes majorly in the Jewish religion.
She completed her early education at the Phoenix Ramp School, Simi Valley, California.
She later enrolled herself at the Southwestern University from where she completed her graduation. From the beginning of her childhood days, she was more interested In modeling rather than in studying. She participated in a lot of competitions and has worked hard. As a teen, King performed a cappella at the Simi Valley Cultural Arts Center’s talent display. Additionally, she collaborated with Agoura’s Stage Door Children’s Theatre.
