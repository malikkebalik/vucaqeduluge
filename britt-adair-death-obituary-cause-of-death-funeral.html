Britt Adair Death: Obituary, Cause Of Death, Funeral

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 29, 2024

Guitarist for The Bad Ideas and Kansas City buyer for Josey Records recently passed away. After a battle with illness, Britt Adair of Kansas City, Missouri, departed Friday night.

Britt Adair was a fantastic vocalist and singer for the well-known band “The Bad Ideas.” Her cause of death was reportedly not immediately confirmed by the family, but claimed declared dead as a result of thyroid condition complications.

The specifics of the funeral services might be revealed later. People in her band and circle are distraught and inconsolable; they send her condolences and tributes and ask God to grant her eternal life.

The multi-talented Britt Adair significantly impacted the Kansas City, Missouri, music and arts scene. She was an accomplished guitarist who performed with the punk group “The Bad Ideas,” where she displayed her skills as a ripping guitarist.

Adair was also an essential aspect of the Kansas City music scene as a buyer at Josey Records Kansas City. She put in countless hours to assist up-and-coming musicians and promote local music.

She obtained her education and perfected her artistic skills at the Kansas City Art Institute, from which she graduated. Adair used her talents for visual storytelling as a camera operator and editor at Firstcut Marketing.

Christian LaBeau had been her husband since February 19, 2017. Britt Adair was a huge presence in the neighborhood, well known for her love of music and her unrelenting dedication to supporting and promoting regional artists.

She was highlighted in a Bridge article a few years back that focused on her achievements as a woman-owned record store proprietor. Her supporters, friends, and family will sincerely miss her contributions to the music and artistic communities of Kansas City.
