Tyler Perry Early Life

Tyler Perry was born on September 13, 1969, in New Orleans, Louisiana. His birth name was Emmitt Perry Jr. He has two elder sisters and one younger brother. His mother was religious and went to church every week, taking Perry with her. His father was a carpenter. He had a problematic childhood as his father beat him a lot. As a result, he suffered a lot from depression.

To get away from his father’s beating, he once even attempted suicide. He also went so far as to change his name from Emmitt to Tyler officially at the age of sixteen to distance himself from his father.

Tyler Perry Career

Writing

He became inspired after watching The Oprah Winfrey Show. There, he got a chance to know about the therapeutic effect of writing on a person. Someone on the show had mentioned that writing could enable a person to work out their problems and give them a sense of relief. This led him to start a career in journalism.

It became his favorite hobby. He wrote the 2006 best-selling book, ‘Don’t Make a Black Woman Take Off Her Earrings.’ It won two Quill Awards. They are ‘Book of the Year’ and ‘Best Humor Book.’ He published his second book, ‘Higher is Waiting‘ on November 14, 2017, which debuted at number 5 on the New York Times Bestseller list.

Plays

He started writing different letters to himself. These letters later became the basis for the musical ‘I Know I’ve Been Changed.’ In 1990, he moved to Atlanta. After two years, I Know I’ve Been Changed was performed at a community theater.

Tyler had expected it to be a hit, but it was a critical as well as a financial failure. But, he did not give up his hope. At the age of 28, he again performed the play in Atlanta’s House of Blue Theatre and at the Fox Theatre. It became prevalent.

Movies & TV Shows

Perry produced his first movie, ‘Diary of a Mad Black Woman,’ a romantic comedy-drama in 2005 with Reuben Cannon. The film successfully grossed $50 million, but critics did not like the movie. After that, he has been involved in several movie projects like ‘Meet the Browns,’ ‘Star Trek,’ ‘Good Deeds,’ ‘Vice,’ etc.

In 2007, he created the sitcom ‘Tyler Perry’s House of Payne’, which he has written, directed, produced, and played as the character of ‘Madea.’ His other TV shows include Tyler Perry’s Meet the Browns, Tyler Perry’s For Better or Worse, Tyler Perry’s The Haves and the Have Nots, etc.

Houses, Properties, and Cars

Well, Perry does not hold back while buying homes. He owns a 25,000 square foot custom-built mansion in Los Angeles. In Atlanta, he owns Tyler Perry Studios and “Avec Chateau,” an almost 17000 square foot custom-built mansion. In French, “Avec Chateau” means “with the home.”

In 2010, Perry bought Dean Gardens for $7.6 million. Its owner was Larry Dean. The house spreads over 32,000 sq. Feet has 15 bedrooms, an 18-hole golf course, and a wedding chapel. In the bathroom, there is a 24 karat gold sink. In 2016, he sold one of his Atlanta area mega-mansions. It set the record as the most expensive house ever sold in Atlanta for $17.5 million.

The next year, he bought another expensive 17000 sq. Foot mansion for $14.5 million. Alfred E.Mann owned it. Besides, he intends to buy a 475 acre former Army base in Atlanta for $75 million to build a movie studio and an office building.

Others refer to the fact that he is nearly 50 years old and still not married. He has dated very few women. He was linked with Tasha Smith as well as Tyra Banks; the latter is the rumor.

Perry has been in a relationship with Gelila Bekele since 2009. They have not married. However, on November 30, 2014, the couple welcomed their son, Aman Tyler Perry.
