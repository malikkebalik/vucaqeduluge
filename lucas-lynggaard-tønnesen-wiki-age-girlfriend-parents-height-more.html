Borgen Actor Lucas Lynggaard Tønnesen Wiki | Bio

Lucas Lynggaard Tønnesen (born July 13, 2000) is a 22 years old Danish actor who is famous for his appearance in the films “Department Q: The Keeper of Lost Causes” and “I Stykker.” In 2022, this young and handsome actor rose to popularity after landing the role of Magnus Nyborg in Adam Price’s hit tv show “Borgen.” It is a political drama that has been running since 2010 and involves actors like Sidse Babett Knudsen, Birgitte Hjort Sørensen, Søren Malling and many more.

As per sources, in 2022, fans will get to see Lynggaard in one more production named “1899,” a horror history drama involving artists like Aneurin Barnard, Emily Beecham, Anton Lesser, Clara Rosager, and Alexandre Willaume.

Career

After the first show, Lucas worked in a film titled “Player,” released in 2013. It was a comedy film directed by the talented Tomas Villum Jensen. He also worked in the Nikolaj Lie Kaas crime drama movie “Department Q: The Keeper of Lost Causes,” a blockbuster project of Mikkel Nørgaard, which did a business of $14 million.

With time things started working for Lynggaard, and he started receiving acting jobs without any auditions. From 2015 to 2019, he performed in “Tidsrejsen,” “The Rain,” “I Stykker,” and “A Favor.” Besides shows/movies, he also did a few tv commercials, mainly for Danish brands.

After working years as a child artist, Lynggaard took a break for a year and then returned with a romantic short movie named “Sail On, My Love.” As per our sources, it is possible that we could get to see him portraying a lead character very soon.
